01 — Featured

A project worth leading with

Groundwork In development

A deal-sourcing platform for Manhattan multifamily that turns public records into an owner-by-owner pipeline — ACRIS review, LLC tracing, debt history, and a built-in CRM.

Problem
Sourcing off-market deals in NYC means hours of manually stitching together ACRIS filings, LLC paper trails, and old mortgage documents — for every single property.
Approach
Pull it all into one place: automated ACRIS document review, LLC ownership tracing to find the actual decision-maker, and debt-history research to spot owners likely to sell.
What I'm building
A web app that turns an address into a full owner profile — recorded documents, the people behind the LLC, and loan history — flowing straight into a CRM built for canvassing.
Result
In progress. A live link and walkthrough will land right here when it ships.
